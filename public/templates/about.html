About the DoJ Chatbot

The Department of Justice (DoJ), under the Ministry of Law & Justice, Government of India, is responsible for vital judiciary functions across the country. In addition to implementing significant infrastructure projects for the judiciary, the DoJ is involved in fast-tracking sensitive cases, providing legal aid, and expanding e-court services.

To improve access to justice, a virtual assistant chatbot has been developed for the DoJ website. This chatbot is designed to assist users by providing quick and easy access to information on:

  • Various divisions of the DoJ
  • Judicial appointments and vacancies
  • Pendency of cases through the National Judicial Data Grid (NJDG)
  • Paying traffic violation fines
  • Live streaming of court cases
  • eFiling and ePayment processes
  • Fast track courts
  • Tele Law services and eCourts Services Mobile App
  • Current status of court cases

The chatbot will continuously learn and improve over time, ensuring users receive accurate and efficient support while handling large datasets, which enhances user interactions.
